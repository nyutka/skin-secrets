Micro-needling

The Future of Youthful Skin with Micro-needling

Micro-needling procedure use the with thirty six microneedles to create tiny and invisible perforations into the top layer of the skin. The skin then jumpstarts its natural healing process and produces collagen and elastin to repair these perforations. This process of creating collagen is completely natural – it is your skin way of regenerating. Your skin naturally becomes firmer and retains its own elasticity.

Micro-needling /Rejuvapen/ treatment not only rejuvenates, lifts, tightens, stimulates collagen production and deliver skin product but also and can reduce:

  • Acne Scars
  • Fine Lines and Wrinkles
  • Skin Laxity
  • Pore Size
  • Texture, Tone and Color
  • Stretch Marks
  • Traumatic Scars
  • Sun Damage
  • Photo Aging
  • Hyperpigmentation

We have also started using micro-needling /Rejuvapen/ for Melasma, which can’t be treated with lasers.

The unique design of Rejuvapen allows for individualized settings, which makes our patients feel comfortable. The treatment itself feels a bit like sandpaper going over the skin. Some area can be more sensitive than other. During the treatment we provide patients with a topical numbing cream. There may be some very minor bleeding during the treatment and occasionally mild bruising can occur.

After treatment most patients look as though they have a moderate to severe sunburn and your skin may feel warm and tighter than usual. Most patients will notice healthier looking skin right away. Full healing will take up to 7 days to reveal brighter, clearer skin. We instruct our patients to using gentle products and sunscreen.

The number of treatments will be determined by each individuals goals and the health of the skin. Usually we recommend 6-8 sessions at 4-6 weeks intervals.
