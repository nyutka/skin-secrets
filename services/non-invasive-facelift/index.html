Non-Invasive Facelift

Non-Invasive Facelift (Laser 360) is an exciting new aesthetic laser treatment to rejuvenate your skin. The device that delivers Laser 360™ Non-Invasive Facelift is the Harmony XL Pro™System from Alma lasers which is FDA approved to correct skin discoloration, texture, tone, scars, and loose skin.

If you are not satisfied with the results from topical creams but are not yet ready to undergo plastic surgery, the Non-invasive facelift (Laser 360) is a great way to improve sagging skin and tone, along with sun damage, fine line and wrinkles.

At Svetlana’s Skin Secrets a multidisciplinary approach is utilized, integrating a mix of advanced cutting-edge technology with unique technique, resulting in healthier appearing skin.

 

How Laser 360 Works

During each session, you may have treatments that involve more than one of the Laser 360 technologies. Your exact treatment regimen will depend on your individual skin condition.

Signs of aging, sun damage, age spots, redness, broken capillaries, fine lines, wrinkles, superficial acne scars, enlarged pores, freckles, uneven pigmentation will be removed, new collagen generated and sagging skin lifted in just 60 days.

To provide complete skin rejuvenation Non-invasive facelift (Laser 360) includes:

Photofacial/AFT/IPL for color. Pulses of light even out your skin color and reduce tiny broken blood vessels and other color irregularities.

Skin Tightening/ST for sagging, loose skin. Deep heating helps firms up any loose skin so you regain a more youthful, contoured appearance.

Skin Resurfacing with iPixel for texture and tone. This precision laser creates thousands of microscopic perforations, which stimulate new collagen to grow. The new collagen improves your skin’s texture and tone to smooth fine lines and wrinkles. The skin around each of these perforations remains intact, which allows the skin’s top layer to heal from the edge of these tiny holes very rapidly.

These procedures require little or no “down” recovery time. You can have a treatment and can usually return to work immediately, certainly by the following day.

To maintain the healthy glow that Laser 360 can bring, clients may choose to come back periodically for touch-up treatments.

For long-lasting results 4-6 treatments are required spaced about two weeks apart.
