Photofacial

How does Photofacial /IPL/AFT work?
The Harmony Dye-VL module combines two trusted technologies, Advanced Fluorescence Technology (AFT) and INTENSE PULSED LIGHT (IPL) to create the new “Gold Standard” for treatment of a variety of skin conditions:
  • Vascular Lesions
  • Rosacea
  • Redness
  • Angiomas
  • Spider Veins
  • Pigment Lesions
  • Hyper-pigmentation
  • Age Spots
  • Brown Spots
  • Sun Damage
  • Freckles
  • Dull complexions
AFT and IPL works using a system of selective pulsed light energy that precisely absorbed by the hemoglobin in the vascular lesions, and the melanin (dark pigment) in the pigmented lesions without damaging healthy skin.
The heat created by AFT and IPL damages the vessel or pigmented lesion, and the body begins its natural healing process, resulting in rejuvenation of the skin. The lesion will become darker initially and then flake off or be absorbed by the body.

 

How long does it take?

Each photofacial session lasts 15-30 minutes. The treated area will be cleansed and a cooling gel applied to the skin. The light pulses are then delivered, improving the look and texture of the treated area.

Once the process is complete the cooling gel is removed and skin is cleansed.

Is it painful?

A mild amount of discomfort can be expected. The level of discomfort depends on how well you tolerate pain. The treatment is similar to the feel of snapping a rubber band against your skin.

What is the skin’s appearance after the treatment?

The appearance of the skin will change over the first 5-7 days after treatment. In the case of sun damage, brown spots, age spots and pigmentation, the spots will typically look darker and may get a crust on them which may last up to 10 days. Approximately 10 days after treatment the spots will begin to clear up and the skin will appear more even in tone.

Though the treatment does not restrict your activity. It is advised that you avoid direct sunlight until you reach that 10 day window after treatment.

How much will it cost?

The cost of treatment varies with each person and will depend on both the severity of the condition and the size of the area being treated.

As always, Svetlana Skin Secret’s offers customized solutions and a free consultation to help determine the best treatment for you.

When will I see results?

As soon as the first treatment is clear you may see noticeable improvements. Each treatment produces a gradual improvement. Some clients may require several treatments to get the desired results.

You can expect to see your skin become smoother and sun spots to fade. Your skin will appear more even-toned, healthy and youthful.

This treatment was designed to target the specific area. Areas surrounded that area will not be impacted by the treatment.

 

Will it take more than one appointment?

You can expect approximately 4-6 AFT/IPL treatment sessions for maximum results, 3-4 weeks apart.

After treatment you may resume daily activities immediately.

Your skin may appear red, tiny capillaries may appear more visible and pigmented spots may look darker after treatment. This will gradually fade over the next few hours to days.

Do I need to take any precautions?

Yes. Limit sun exposure in the weeks leading up to and after the treatment to achieve the best results.

This includes the use of retinoid products and sun tanning of any type.

Be sure to cleanse with gentle cleansers, apply moisturizer and use SPF 30 or above after treatment.

Will it damage my skin?

Skin will appear even-toned, healthy and youthful. Because the treatment was developed to specifically heat the intended target spot, the surrounding areas will not experience any damage or discomfort. Skin will appear even-toned, healthy and youthful.
