Enlarged Pores

Large /Enlarged/ Pores are a very frustrating condition that affects both men and women, especially those with oily or combination skin.

Enlarged pores can be related to genetics, excessive sun exposure, improper skin care regime, age and other various influences.

Pores become more prominent when they are dilated, clogged with dirt and bacteria otherwise known as blackheads. Oil within those pores begins to accumulate, causing the pore’s diameter to expand – making them noticeable.

Although there is no cure to permanently “shrink” enlarged pores, we offer various treatments that can drastically minimize the appearance, making them less noticeable.
